# 30-Day Hackathon Sponsorship Plan

Goal: use hackathons and builder events to reach active AI builders, support realistic agent projects, and create early adoption paths for SandBase.

This is not a logo-placement sponsorship strategy.

SandBase should sponsor where it can help builders ship better tool-using agents.

## Positioning

```text
SandBase helps hackathon teams build production-ready AI agents with sandboxed runtime, safe tool execution, model routing, APIs, and distributed compute.
```

## Ideal Events

Prioritize events with:

- AI agents, AI infra, devtools, automation, or open-source themes
- builders who write code, not only prompt demos
- remote or global participation
- sponsor tracks or prize categories
- active organizer communication
- room for technical workshops, office hours, or credits

## Sponsorship Offer

Starter package:

- SandBase credits for participants
- sandboxed runtime / API access for selected teams
- starter templates
- office hours for teams building agents with tools
- prize track: Best Production-Ready Agent
- post-event showcase for strong projects

## 30-Day Arc

| Week | Theme | Goal |
|------|-------|------|
| Week 1 | Event discovery | Find 30 relevant hackathons / AI builder events |
| Week 2 | Offer testing | Contact 10 organizers with a clear sponsor/support package |
| Week 3 | Partnership follow-up | Convert 2-3 conversations into concrete next steps |
| Week 4 | Launch first support package | Prepare credits, templates, landing page, and post-event follow-up workflow |

## Daily Operating Loop

Use the [Daily SOP](daily-sop.md) as the repeatable operating checklist.

1. Find 3-5 events or communities.
2. Score fit.
3. Pick 1-2 organizers to contact.
4. Prepare a personalized message.
5. Follow up on existing conversations.
6. Record status, blocker, and next action.

## Event Scoring

| Field | Score |
|-------|-------|
| AI agent relevance | 1-5 |
| Builder quality | 1-5 |
| Sponsor accessibility | 1-5 |
| Timeline fit | 1-5 |
| SandBase fit | 1-5 |

High-priority event:

```text
AI agent relevance >= 4
Builder quality >= 4
Sponsor accessibility >= 3
Timeline fit >= 3
SandBase fit >= 4
```

```text
If you are open to it, I would be happy to share a lightweight sponsor/support package for teams building tool-using agents.
```

## Do Not Do

- Do not pay for broad logo placement without builder access.
- Do not sponsor events where teams are not building technical projects.
- Do not overpromise credits, infra limits, or support availability.
- Do not publish organizer conversations without permission.
- Do not treat hackathons as backlink farms.
