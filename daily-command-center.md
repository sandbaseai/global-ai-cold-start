# Daily Command Center

This is the daily operating dashboard for Global AI Cold Start.

Use it to decide what to do each day across three tracks:

1. Growth foundation
2. Overseas builders outreach
3. Hackathon sponsorship

## Daily Time Budget

| Track | Time | Outcome |
|-------|------|---------|
| Growth foundation | 30-60 min | Publish, distribute, or improve one public trust asset |
| Overseas builders outreach | 60-90 min | Find leads, send useful messages, learn from builders |
| Hackathon sponsorship | 45-75 min | Find events, contact organizers, prepare support offers |

If time is limited, use this priority:

1. Reply to existing conversations.
2. Do one high-quality outreach action.
3. Improve one public asset.
4. Record what happened.

## Daily Checklist

### 1. Growth Foundation

- [ ] Check X, LinkedIn, Discord, Peerlist, Dev.to notifications.
- [ ] Reply where there is a real technical conversation.
- [ ] Share one useful product or technical update if there is something real to say.
- [ ] Record new public links, mentions, or learnings.

### 2. Overseas Builders Outreach

- [ ] Find 10 relevant builders or projects.
- [ ] Score leads.
- [ ] Pick 3-5 high-fit leads.
- [ ] Prepare personalized outreach.
- [ ] Send only approved, specific messages.
- [ ] Record replies and follow-ups.

See: [Overseas Builders Outreach SOP](overseas-builders-outreach/daily-sop.md)

### 3. Hackathon Sponsorship

- [ ] Find 3-5 relevant hackathons or AI builder events.
- [ ] Score events.
- [ ] Pick 1-2 high-fit events.
- [ ] Prepare organizer outreach.
- [ ] Record follow-up dates.

See: [Hackathon Sponsorship SOP](hackathon-sponsorship/daily-sop.md)

## Rules

- No mass outreach.
- No private account details in public logs.
- No publishing private conversations without permission.
- No paid sponsorship or directory submission without explicit approval.
- Public content should help builders, not just promote SandBase.
